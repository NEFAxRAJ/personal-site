Travelling

"Travel broadens the mind"

I dont know anyone who does not love vacation. And for me vacation is nothing but escaping the mundane life and travelling. Mountains, beaches, forests, desserts and islands, everything sound so exciting. Am always on toes for a trip and if its a roadtrip nothing could have been better. I would never like to bore someone telling my experiences but rather advice everyone to travel and have their one of a kind experience.

Roadtrips are the perfect kind of travelling I love. Going throug the local streets and interacting with people, exploring the place on your own has a different feeling altogether. Seeing the scenes change from rustic towns to villages to metro cities, its just a rollercoaster of emotions for me. The love for highways and roads thats unexplainable.
With only a fraction of India explored, I cant wait to see every bit of my country.
Some notable Roadtrips were-

  • Kolkata to Bhutan(1800+km)
  • Kolkata to Indo-Pak border at Jaisalmer(5300+km in 12 days)
